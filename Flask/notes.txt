4 main types of databases:

1. Relational/SQL databases - databases > tables > rows > columns (MySQL, PostgreSQL, SQLite)
2. NoSQL/Document databases - databases > collections > documents (JSON-like structures) (MongoDB, CouchDB)
3. Graph databases - databases > nodes > edges  (Neo4j, ArangoDB)
4. Key-Value databases - databases > Keys > Values (Redis, DynamoDB)

5. Blob stores - databases > blobs (binary large objects) (Amazon S3, Google Cloud Storage)
